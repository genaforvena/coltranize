Coltranizer

Finds II‑V‑I progressions on this page and replaces them with Coltrane changes.

About

Coltranizer applies the harmonic substitution system popularised by John Coltrane ("Coltrane changes") to chord progressions found in any web page — tabs, lyrics, sheet music — and lets you hear the music in a new light.
